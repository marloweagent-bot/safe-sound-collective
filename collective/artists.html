Artist & Venue Partnerships

We're building a network of artists and venues committed to protecting hearing. This program is currently in development — below is our working framework.

For Artists

Join artists who are adding hearing protection to their technical riders and shows.

  • Sound Rider: Add hearing protection requirements to your technical rider
  • Provide or promote hearing protection at your shows
  • Use your platform to normalize hearing safety
  • Access to Safe Sound Collective resources and branding

For Venues

Become a certified Safe Sound venue with tools and training.

  • Decibel monitoring and enforced noise limits
  • Hearing protection available for all attendees
  • Staff training on hearing safety
  • Safe Sound Certified badge and marketing support

Partnership Tiers (Draft)

Founding Artists

Equity partnership for artists who help shape the program from day one.

Collective Artists

Product partnerships and revenue share for artists who join the movement.

Certified Venues

Venues that meet Safe Sound standards get certified status and support.
