Our Mission

Why We Exist

Hearing loss in music isn't inevitable. It's preventable. We're here to make protection the norm, not the exception.

Normalize Protection

Earplugs have been uncool for too long. We're changing that by making hearing protection something artists and fans are proud to wear.

Protect Longevity

Careers shouldn't end because of preventable damage. We're helping artists protect the ears that create the music we love.

Shift the Culture

When the artists fans admire wear protection, fans follow. The Collective is the cultural engine driving that change.
