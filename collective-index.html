Our Mission

We're Solving a Major Epidemic of Sound

1.1 billion people are at risk of hearing loss. It's 100% preventable. We're building the education, tools, and partnerships to protect hearing in the modern age.

Our Approach

Three Phases to Change the Soundscape

We're tackling noise-induced hearing loss systematically — starting with education, expanding through partnerships, and scaling to protect everyone.

01
Now Live

Educate Audiences

We built the Safe Sound Guide — a free, interactive resource teaching people about sound, hearing damage, and protection. No jargon, no fear tactics. Just clear science and practical advice for music lovers, DJs, musicians, and anyone in loud environments.

Explore the Guide →
02
Coming Soon

Artists & Venues

We're partnering with artists to add hearing protection requirements to their technical riders. We're certifying venues with decibel monitoring, noise limits, and available hearing protection. When the people who create the sound lead by example, fans follow.

03
Future

Workplaces & Beyond

The epidemic extends past concerts — construction sites, factories, airports, subway platforms. We'll bring the same education-first, partnership-driven approach to industrial settings, workplaces, and commuter hubs. Everywhere sound exists, protection should too.

Hearing Loss is the Only Major Health Issue That's 100% Preventable — Yet Almost No One Prevents It

We're changing that. One guide, one artist, one venue at a time.
