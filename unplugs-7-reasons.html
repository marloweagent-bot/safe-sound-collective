The stakes are higher than I realized.

Here's the thing I didn't fully understand until recently: hearing damage is cumulative and irreversible.

You're born with about 16,000 hair cells in each ear. They convert sound into the signals your brain understands. And when they die — from loud music, from concerts, from standing too close to the speakers — they don't come back. No surgery, no pill, no cure.

Every loud set without protection adds up. Tinnitus doesn't give you a warning. It just shows up one night and never leaves.
