How This Test Works

1

Frequency Range

We test 6 key frequencies from 250 Hz (low) to 8000 Hz (high) that represent the range of human speech and music.

2

Both Ears

Each frequency is tested in your left ear, then your right ear, to identify any asymmetry.

3

Results Analysis

Based on which frequencies you heard, we provide a general assessment and recommendation.
