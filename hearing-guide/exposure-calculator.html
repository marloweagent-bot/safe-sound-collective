How This Calculator Works

This calculator uses the NIOSH recommended exposure limit (REL) formula. The daily dose is calculated as a percentage of your safe daily exposure limit.

The formula: At 85 dB, you have 8 hours of safe exposure. Every 3 dB increase halves that time. Your dose accumulates throughout the day‚Äîmultiple exposures add up.

100% = your limit. Going over 100% means you're exposed to more noise than recommended and risk hearing damage. Under 50% is ideal.

Understanding Your Results

  • 0-50% (Green): You're well within safe limits. Great job protecting your hearing!
  • 50-100% (Yellow): Approaching your limit. Consider reducing exposure or using protection.
  • Over 100% (Red): You've exceeded safe limits. Hearing damage may occur with repeated exposure at this level.
